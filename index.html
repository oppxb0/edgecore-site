The Problem

Three Problems
Most Organizations
Don't Catch in Time

  • 01
    The program is behind and over budget A technology initiative that started with a clear plan has stalled — schedule slipping, costs climbing, no one with authority to course-correct.
  • 02
    Infrastructure built for where you were The network, the systems, the architecture — designed for the business two years ago. Now you're scaling and it's showing cracks under load.
  • 03
    Vendors costing more than they deliver Contracts that made sense at signing now feel misaligned. Escalation paths don't work. Accountability has eroded. Enterprise rates, mid-market results.

    Post-acquisition technology chaos PE-backed growth means inherited infrastructure from acquired businesses — inconsistent, undocumented, and carrying risk that hasn't been assessed.

About

Edge Core Advisors was founded by Paul Byrnes after a 25-year career at Target Corporation — directing infrastructure programs at a scale most organizations will never encounter. 2,000+ store locations. 50+ distribution centers. $100M+ capital programs executed on time and on budget.
