About

Built on 25 Years
of Ops Reality

Edge Core Advisors was founded by Paul Byrnes after a 25-year career at Target Corporation — building and running infrastructure at a scale most businesses will never encounter. 2,000+ store locations. 50+ distribution centers. $100M+ capital programs.

That career was a long lesson in one thing: the gap between what technology can do and what operations teams are actually using it for. Most of the time savings we found at enterprise scale came from replacing manual work that nobody had gotten around to automating yet.

That gap is even wider at small and mid-size businesses — and the tools to close it have never been more accessible. That's what Edge Core does.
